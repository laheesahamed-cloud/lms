Dark mode scan

Four dark theme directions for xyndrome.

I scanned the current React/Vite LMS tokens and dark screenshots. The current mode is readable, but the background, cards, quiz panels, and sidebar are too close in value, so the whole product feels like one dense navy sheet.

Use clearer surface steps: page, sidebar, card, input, selected state.
Keep accents medical and useful, not decorative glow.
Make quiz answer states and progress bars pop without screaming.
Preserve the current token model so implementation stays contained.

Four suggestions from the scan

Pick one accent family.The current dark mode mixes blue, violet, cyan, and purple; tighten it to one primary accent and one support accent.
Raise card contrast.Use at least four visible surface steps so dashboard cards and quiz panels do not blend into the page.
Reduce colored glow.Use borders and fills for state, not heavy glow. It will look more clinical and less game-like.
Keep semantic colors stable.Success, warning, danger, and selected answers should remain instantly recognizable across themes.
